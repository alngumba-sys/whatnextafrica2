# 🎥 3-Minute Platform Walkthrough Script

**Total Duration:** 3 minutes  
**Speaking Pace:** Moderate (about 150 words/minute)  
**Total Words:** ~450 words

---

## 📍 TIMING MARKERS

- **[0:00-0:15]** Introduction + QR Code
- **[0:15-0:45]** Platform Overview
- **[0:45-2:00]** Core Features Demo
- **[2:00-2:45]** Key Highlights
- **[2:45-3:00]** Conclusion + QR Code

---

## 🎬 THE SCRIPT

### [0:00-0:15] INTRODUCTION
**[Show QR code on screen]**

> "Welcome! This is the Kenya Government Administrative Hierarchy Management Platform – a comprehensive system managing **2,464 officers** across **47 counties**. 
>
> Scan this QR code to try it yourself. Let me show you what it can do."

**Timing note:** 15 seconds | ~40 words

---

### [0:15-0:45] PLATFORM OVERVIEW
**[Login and show dashboard]**

> "I'm logging in as a Cabinet Secretary with full system access. 
>
> The dashboard immediately shows our entire administrative structure: 2,464 officers spanning 6 levels – from Cabinet Secretary down to Assistant Commissioners – organized in a dual-track system covering County Administration and Secretariat operations.
>
> You can see pending transfers, active officers, and real-time reporting status."

**Actions:**
- Login as cs.interior
- Point to key statistics
- Hover over charts

**Timing note:** 30 seconds | ~75 words

---

### [0:45-1:15] ROLE-BASED ACCESS
**[Navigate to Officers section]**

> "One powerful feature is role-based access. Each user sees only what's relevant to their jurisdiction.
>
> As Cabinet Secretary, I see all 47 counties. But if I log in as a Regional Coordinator, they'd only see their region. County Commissioners see their county. This ensures data privacy and efficient management.
>
> Here I can browse officers, filter by region, county, or role, and view complete profiles with service history and transfer records."

**Actions:**
- Show officers list
- Apply a filter (e.g., Nairobi region)
- Click on an officer profile
- Scroll through profile

**Timing note:** 30 seconds | ~80 words

---

### [1:15-2:00] TRANSFER MANAGEMENT
**[Navigate to Transfers, create one]**

> "Transfer management includes multi-level approval workflows. Let me create a transfer request.
>
> I'll transfer this Deputy County Commissioner from Westlands to Kibra. The system automatically identifies the approval chain: first the immediate supervisor, then County Commissioner, then Regional Coordinator.
>
> Each approver can review details, approve or reject with comments, and the system maintains a complete audit trail. Everything is transparent and accountable.
>
> The platform also tracks transfer history, so you can see every officer's movement over time."

**Actions:**
- Click "New Transfer"
- Select an officer
- Choose new location
- Show approval chain
- Submit (or show existing transfer)

**Timing note:** 45 seconds | ~100 words

---

### [2:00-2:30] AI RESHUFFLE & LEAVE
**[Show AI Reshuffle and Leave sections briefly]**

> "We've integrated AI-powered features. The reshuffle tool analyzes years of service, performance, and regional balance to suggest optimal officer placements – saving hundreds of hours of manual planning.
>
> Officers can also manage leave applications directly through the platform. Apply for leave, check balances, and supervisors can approve requests while viewing the leave calendar to ensure adequate staffing."

**Actions:**
- Click on AI Reshuffle
- Show the interface (don't run simulation)
- Navigate to Leave section
- Show leave balance or application form

**Timing note:** 30 seconds | ~70 words

---

### [2:30-2:45] ANALYTICS & REPORTS
**[Navigate to Reports/Analytics]**

> "The platform provides comprehensive analytics – officer distribution by region, transfer trends, leave statistics, and performance dashboards. All reports can be filtered, exported to Excel or PDF, and help leadership make data-driven decisions.
>
> This supports Kenya Vision 2030's governance reforms for transparency and accountability."

**Actions:**
- Show reports dashboard
- Display a chart or visualization
- Hover over export options

**Timing note:** 15 seconds | ~50 words

---

### [2:45-3:00] CONCLUSION
**[Return to dashboard, show QR code full screen]**

> "This platform delivers complete officer management, multi-level workflows, AI optimization, and real-time analytics – all supporting efficient, transparent public service delivery.
>
> **[Show QR code]**
>
> Scan this QR code to explore the platform yourself. Thank you!"

**Actions:**
- Show main dashboard briefly
- Display QR code prominently
- Hold for 5+ seconds

**Timing note:** 15 seconds | ~45 words

## 🎬 DELIVERY TIPS

### Pacing:
- **Speak clearly** - Don't rush
- **Pause briefly** - After showing each section
- **Vary your tone** - Enthusiasm for features
- **Slow down** - For important numbers

### Voice:
- **Confident** - You know this system
- **Professional** - But friendly
- **Clear articulation** - Every word matters
- **Natural pauses** - Breathe!

### Timing:
- **Don't rush** - If you run 10-15 seconds over, that's fine
- **Practice** - Do 2-3 run-throughs
- **Use timer** - Check your pace
- **Adjust** - Skip a feature if running long

## 🎤 PRACTICE ROUTINE

### Before Recording:

1. **Read aloud 3 times** - Get comfortable with words
2. **Time yourself** - Should be 2:50-3:10
3. **Record practice run** - Listen back
4. **Adjust pace** - Too fast? Slow down
5. **Mark difficult words** - Practice pronunciation

### Common Mistakes to Avoid:

- ❌ Speaking too fast (rushing)
- ❌ Monotone voice (boring)
- ❌ Skipping the QR code time
- ❌ Not pausing between sections
- ❌ Saying "um" or "uh"

### If You Make a Mistake:

- ✅ Pause for 3 seconds
- ✅ Take a breath
- ✅ Restart that sentence
- ✅ Continue naturally
