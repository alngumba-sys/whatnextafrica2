## 🎨 Visual Design Notes

### Colors
- Background: Clean white or light gray
- Accent: Kenya colors (Green, Red, Black)
- Text: Dark gray for readability
- Highlights: Blue for interactive elements

### QR Code Placement
- **Scenes 1-2:** Full screen or prominent
- **Scenes 3-10:** Small in top-right corner (150x150px)
- **Scene 11:** Full screen or large
- Always visible for at least 5 seconds

### Transitions
- Fade between major sections
- Smooth scroll within same page
- No jarring cuts

### Text Overlays
- Add section titles (e.g., "Transfer Management")
- Highlight key numbers (2,464, 47 counties)
- Use lower thirds for definitions

### Mouse/Cursor
- Smooth, deliberate movements
- Highlight important buttons/fields
- Circle cursor for emphasis (optional)

---

## 📱 Mobile Viewing Note

Since viewers will scan QR code from video:
- Keep QR code large (minimum 300x300px on screen)
- High contrast background
- Show for minimum 5 seconds
- Add text: "Scan with phone camera"

---

## 🎬 Production Tips

1. **Zoom levels:** Use 100% for most shots, zoom in for details
2. **Pacing:** Allow 2-3 seconds per screen before speaking
3. **Focus:** Highlight active elements (cursor, click animations)
4. **Consistency:** Keep UI at same position throughout
5. **Clean data:** Use realistic but professional names/data
