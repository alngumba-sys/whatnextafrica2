# 🎤 CONVERSATIONAL SCRIPT - CHEAT SHEET

**Style:** Natural talk while browsing  
**Duration:** 3 min  
**Approach:** Show & tell, not formal presentation

---

## Quick Flow

**[QR 5sec]** "Welcome! Scan this QR. Let me show you what this platform does."

**[Login → Dashboard]** "Logging in as Cabinet Secretary... managing **2,464 officers across 47 counties**. Six-level hierarchy from Cabinet Secretary down to Assistant Commissioners. Dual-track: County Admin + Secretariat."

**[Officers section]** "**Role-based access** – I see all officers, but Regional Coordinators only see their region, County Commissioners see their county. Secure and efficient. Can filter by region, county, role. Click any officer – complete profile, posting, supervisor, service years, **transfer history, leave balance**. Everything in one place."

**[Transfers]** "Creating a transfer – moving DC from Westlands to Kibra. System **auto-detects approval chain**: supervisor → County Commissioner → Regional Coordinator. Each approver reviews, approves/rejects with comments. **Complete audit trail** – total transparency. Track entire lifecycle."

**[Leave]** "Officers apply for leave here. System checks balance, no overlaps, routes to supervisor. Supervisors see team calendar for adequate staffing."

**[AI Reshuffle]** "**AI-powered innovation** – analyzes service years, performance, regional balance, suggests optimal placements. Smart recommendations in minutes, not weeks."

**[Reports]** "Comprehensive analytics – officer distribution, transfer trends, leave stats, dashboards. **Export to Excel/PDF**. Real-time updates for **data-driven decisions**."

**[Wrap]** "Platform supports **Kenya Vision 2030** – transparency, accountability, efficient public service. Managing 2,464 officers, 47 counties, AI optimization, multi-level workflows, real-time analytics."

**[QR 7sec]** "Scan to explore yourself. Thanks!"

---

## 💡 Remember

- **Talk while clicking** – describe what you're doing
- **Sound natural** – like showing a friend
- **Use:** "you can see", "let me show you", "watch this"
- **Pause naturally** when screens load
- **Don't memorize** – use your own words
- **Show enthusiasm** for cool features!
