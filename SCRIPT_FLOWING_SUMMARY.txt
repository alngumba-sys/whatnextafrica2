## 🎯 What Makes It Flow

1. **"And"** connects related ideas
2. **"So"** transitions to new actions  
3. **"Now"** introduces new features
4. **"Let me"** when showing something
5. **No periods** - only commas between thoughts
6. **Run-on sentences** that sound natural when spoken
