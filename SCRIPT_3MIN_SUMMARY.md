## 🎤 Delivery Tips

### Voice & Pace:
- Speak **clearly** - Don't rush
- **Moderate pace** - Not too fast, not too slow
- **Pause** briefly between sections
- **Vary tone** - Show enthusiasm for features
- **Smile** - Even off-camera, it improves your voice!

### Common Mistakes:
- ❌ Speaking too fast (most common!)
- ❌ Monotone voice
- ❌ Not showing QR code long enough
- ❌ Saying "um" or "uh"
- ❌ Not pausing between sections

### If You Make a Mistake:
1. Pause for 3 seconds
2. Take a breath
3. Restart that sentence
4. Continue naturally
5. You'll edit it out later!

## ✂️ Optional Cuts (If Running Long)

If you find yourself running over 3 minutes, you can trim:

1. **Shorten officer profile demo** - Just show it, don't scroll
2. **Condense AI section** - Mention it exists, don't show interface
3. **Skip leave details** - Just say "and leave management"
4. **Shorter intro** - Skip "Let me show you what it can do"

These cuts can save 20-30 seconds total.
