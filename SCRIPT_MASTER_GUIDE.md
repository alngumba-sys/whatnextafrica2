## 💡 Pro Tips by Style

### For Formal Delivery:
- Practice reading aloud 3 times
- Use timer to hit exact timing
- Pause deliberately between sections
- Clear, measured pace
- Professional tone throughout

### For Conversational Delivery:

- Show genuine enthusiasm
- Be yourself!
